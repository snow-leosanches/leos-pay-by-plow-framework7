We want to address all your needs

We want your experience with Leo's Pay By Plow Credit Line to feel secure. Below are answers that often come up — contact us in a full product for more.

How do I withdraw my money?

In supported regions, you may withdraw at partner bank branches with valid ID — demo placeholder (see PayJoy Cash for reference).

How much money can I withdraw?

Any amount up to your available line — minimums may apply (e.g. 100 in local currency) in real programs.

Can I cancel or modify a withdrawal request?

For security, some programs don't allow canceling a pending request — you wait until it expires (e.g. 7 days) before starting a new one.

How soon will my withdrawal be available?

Your withdrawal code may appear in the app within 24 hours — illustrative.

Where can I make my payments?

Convenience stores, bank transfers, or in-app references — choose what suits you in the app; reference codes may refresh weekly.

Can I use the Credit Line if I haven't used Leo's Pay By Plow before?

You can apply as a new user — download the app and complete the flow. Some real programs limit certain offers to specific device brands (e.g. Samsung-only promos).
